Life breding base

Yijia Ding

brand design

Medium:modeling, posters

Ruwon, a life incubator, was born due to the declining desire of young people to have children. Inspired by the process of self-pollination of flowers, the building consists of stamens of different structures that mimic pollination-producing organisms. At the same time, it is also a miniature womb, conveying the idea of self-asexual or unisexual life.
